Navigate To Create: IM Lab

Art Center - C3

About

This website serves as a visual guide to help you navigate through the C3 building at NYU Abu Dhabi and reach the Interactive Media (IM) Lab. The central feature is an interactive map where you can click on starting points to receive step-by-step directions to the IM Lab.

Designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, this site combines video and interactivity to make sure you're never lost when trying to find the IM Lab. The map markers highlight key areas within the building, and each one offers directions that lead you closer to the creative heart of C3.

Through this project, I wanted to create an easy-to-use tool that helps others seamlessly locate the IM Lab and immerse themselves in the world of interactive media.
